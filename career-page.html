Careers and training

If you would like the opportunity to work as a full-time staff at Envivo Communications, please send a copy of your CV here. Our representatives will get back to you. All our internship programs are sponsored by the Central Bank of Nigeria in partnership with Envivo Communications. All internships will last for 3 years consisting of 1 year training and an optional 2 years employment with Envivo Communications.

Educational Requirements

All candidates must have their first degree certificate and NYSC certificate.
