Who we are.

We are Envivo. An African content distribution network (CDN) company with a mission to digitally transform various sectors across the African continent and globe. The pillars of our digital transformation lie in the areas of education, entertainment, distribution, training and production. We have partnered with some of the best companies globally (CISCO, Ford Foundation, UNITY etc) to drive home a message of change, delivering self-sufficient social impact. We are Envivo and the future is here.
